Frat Track Portal



Contributors: Claire Lee, Stevie Kobori, Mei Wan, Calvin Lui

We used Ruby on Rails, Bootstrap, Google Authentication to build a portal for frats/clubs to use. Their members can all have an account under their frat/club, and adminstrators can track reimbursements, submited rush names, and completed tasks. See the website here. Feel free to sign in with your Google account to play around. See the code here.
